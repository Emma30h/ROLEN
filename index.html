Who we are...

ROLEN is an Argentine construction company, outstanding in the field of architecture, design and engineering branches, it begins to provide services from 2022 throughout the territory of the Argentine Republic with headquarters in the town of Salsacate, Pocho, Córdoba.

The company generates projects with strict standards and commitment to quality, aesthetics and comfort, we take architecture as an art "the art of generating spaces" and hence our slogan "the art of building", we have new technology equipment and always up-to-date in the new construction methods and avant-garde.
